About Deane

Deane Nettles is a graphic designer, photographer, and illustrator. He has a BA in News Journalism and an MFA in Integrated Design from the University of Baltimore and currently freelances for various clients and teaches at numerous colleges and universities in the DC/Baltimore area.

After starting with regional ad agencies, he has freelanced for ad agencies, design studios and direct marketing firms. He has also art directed several publications and was senior art director at August, Lang & Husak in Bethesda.

His strengths are:

  • Reliable “go to person” for knowledge of advertising and graphic design
  • Consistently dependable to deliver
  • Accountable to be ever at the forefront of the field seeking new knowledge, approaches, programs, etc.
  • Patient, determined, a good listener

Deane is a past president and life member of the Art Directors Club of Metropolitan Washington, where he ran the Annual Show, chaired programs and produced a workshop in logo design conducted by Paula Scher. He is an occasional contributor to DC and Baltimore AIGAs.

Since 2006 he has taught at area colleges and universities, including intro courses in graphic design and web design and advanced courses in typography, advertising, branding and front-end web coding.

Deane enjoys working with energetic, committed teams of fellow designers — teams so dedicated they're told to quiet down because they're having too much fun and then enthusiastically continue their discussions after work over pizza and beer.

Deane is married with a wife, a dog and a cat; is currently learning type design and javascript; draws, paints and sculpts; bikes over a thousand miles a year; and dances and teaches swing, waltz, blues and zydeco.
