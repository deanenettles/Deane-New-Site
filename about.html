About Deane

Creating exciting ideas and producing extraordinary results.

Deane Nettles is a graphic designer, photographer, and illustrator. He has a BA in News Journalism from Kent State University and an MFA in Integrated Design from the University of Baltimore. He currently freelances for various clients and teaches at numerous colleges and universities in the DC/Baltimore area.

Nettles began on a college newspaper as a photographer, where he learned newspaper layout. He got his undergraduate degree in news journalism and went back for a degree in advertising design while editing the same student paper. The former editor got him a position working as a production artist at boutique agency Morgan Burchette; from there he became art director at KSK Communications. Since then he has freelanced for ad agencies, design studios, direct marketing firms and publications. He has also been art director at several publications and senior art director at August, Lang & Husak in Bethesda.

Nettles is a past president and life member of the Art Directors Club of Metropolitan Washington, where he ran the Annual Show, chaired programs and produced a workshop in logo design conducted by Paula Scher from Pentagram.

He started teaching in 2006 and has taught intro courses in design and web design and advanced courses in typography, advertising and branding.
